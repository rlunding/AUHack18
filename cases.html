Cases at AUHack

Our sponsors are each hosting a case, where you can compete to make the best solution. These are based in each companie's expertise, and are meant as an inspiration and foundation for your projects here at AUHack18.

Do we need to sign up?

Yes, we would like to know what you're working on. We're walking around the area saturday, asking you what you've chosen (if you've chosen any).

Can we solve several cases at once?

Yes, of course! We're not limiting your creativity or area that you want to focus on.

What do we do if we're not here saturday to be signed up for a case?

If we miss you, or if you're not at the venue, when we're walking around, you can just go to the infodesk - then we'll take care of it!

Water the World - #Hacks4Water
Grundfos
Description
753 million people is without clean drinking water. More than 1 billion liter drinking water is lost in London every day. Help solve this problem.
Technologies provided
  • Big Data - Grundfos Dormitory
    Are you interested in using a data driven approach to energy optimize an apartment or rethink the indoor climate? Get access to year's data from 150 apartments in the Grundfos Dormitory located at Aarhus Ø
  • Entering the Augmented Reality
    In Grundfos we have exploted the new possibilities of augmented reality for training of new employees and commissioning of pump systems. Find your way to merge the physical world with the cyber world by either programming Microsoft HoloLens in Unity's 3D environment or by using the already implemented functionalities
  • Big Data - AQtap Water ATM
    The Grundfos AQtap is an intelligent water ATM addressing reliable and sustainable water supply in the developing world. You get access to a data set from several AQtaps installed in Africa showing water consumption patterns.
  • SCALA2 pump
    SCALA2 is a fully integrated water booster pump providing constant pressure with multiple water sources running at the same time. It packs pump, motor, tank, sensor, drive and non-return valve into one compact unit. This SCALA2 has been enabled with Bluetooth Low Energy for AUHack, giving you the ability to remotely control it and gather data from it. SCALA2 data can thereby tell you a lot of i.e. usage patterns of homeowners.
  • MGE motor
    One of the most efficient motors in the world is the Grundfos MGE. This MGE has been enabled with Bluetooth Low Energy, giving you the ability to remotely control it and gather data from it. The MGE motor is used as driver for a lot of different pumps and will tell you a lot about how a pump is running.
  • Bluetooth Dongle
    You can use your own Bluetooth Enabled HW for communication with our products. Otherwise, an easy connectivity option is our Bluetooth Dongle, which makes your Bluetooth connection seem as a serial port.
Got any questions or technical problems?
Feel free to ask us about anything, any time. Simply come find us at our stand. Our mentors specializes in:
Python machine learning package, Clustering algorithms, Supervised and unsupervised learning, Embedded programming, UX/UI, Digital Businnes Models, Software concept prototypin, IoT, 3D models, text mining and much more.
Associate 3rd party wearables/IoT’s with MobilePay
Danske Bank
Description
+3.7 mio. Danes and Finns use MobilePay. Help us solve the case of associating 3rd party wearable/IoT devices with MobilePay, and make payment more seamless.
Possible cases
  • Make a concept, which use wearables for payment and where the moment of payment is handled by a 3rd party.
  • Develop a 3rd party solution that reads a wearable and uses the input for payment and maybe other things…
  • Develop a mock of our payment app that is able to manage associated wearables, e.g. register, un-register, top up money, limit use and do valuable stuff with previous transactions and other logged events.
Comments on the case
User’s are not always MobilePay users. They could be children or foreigners. How can we include them?

Customers might use the wearable to give 3rd parties access to selected personal data and thus not just payments. How could that be used to create value?
Shaping the Education of Tomorrow
LEGO
Description
Create a fun and engaging learning experience combining LEGO products with other technologies or domains to explore new opportunities and innovate the world of education.
Possible cases
  • Can you combine learning while being physically active with LEGO bricks?    
  • How does data help improve classroom activities, test and assessments? 
  • How can natural language processing be part of the LEGO experience?
  • How can students learn to code by using physical elements or LEGO bricks?   
  • How would you make LEGO Education curriculum content fun and engaging?
  • How can technology and LEGO help improve student communication skills?
  • How could image recognition be part of a digital learning experience?

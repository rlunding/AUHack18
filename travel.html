Location

The address of the venue building is:
Finlandsgade 21, 8200 Aarhus N, Denmark

You will recognize the entrance by AUHack signs outside

Train

Take the train to Aarhus Hovedbanegård.
You can then take a bus or walk to the venue.

Car

Navigate to Finlandsgade 21, 8200 Aarhus N, Denmark

Park your car somewhere, preferable on the roof of Storcenter Nord

Plane

You have 4 options: Copenhagen, Billund, Tirstrup, Aalborg. Billund and Copenhagen are likely preferred, since they have most connecting flights.

If you arrive at Copenhagen Airport take the train to Aarhus H and then bus 2A to the venue, link.

If you arrive at Billund Airport take the bus 912X til Aarhus then bus 2A to the venue, link.

Questions

If you have any questions regarding travel, please contact us at contact@auhack.org.
